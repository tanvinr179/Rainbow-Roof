About Us

Do you have to pretend to be siblings to rent an apartment? Do you have to hide your identity to feel safe in your society?

We believe safe housing is a fundamental right and everyone should have access to it. Rainbow Roof provides safe housing options for the LGBT+ community. We assess each of the properties and vet the societies as well as the surroundings for safety. We assist with all types of accomodations including:

  • Buying a propery
  • Renting a property
  • PG and Sharing accomodations
  • Hostel accomodations
  • Service Apartments
